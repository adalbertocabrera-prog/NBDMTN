# Origin

Before the world understood the symbols, there was only the signal.

NBDMTN began as a hidden archive of discoveries, artifacts, and visions connected by one mystery: something ancient was trying to be remembered.

The first artifacts, NBDMTN-001 and NBDMTN-002, are not just images. They are records. Each one carries a fragment of a larger story.

This universe exists to document the rise of those fragments before they become legend.
