# Manifesto

NBDMTN is built on a simple belief:

Ideas become legends when they survive their creators.

Every symbol, artifact, image, and story in this repository exists to preserve a discovery.

The goal is not to follow trends.
The goal is to create something that people can still explore years from now.

Questions are valued more than answers.
Mystery is valued more than certainty.
Discovery is valued more than ownership.

NBDMTN is an archive of possibilities.

The search never ends.
