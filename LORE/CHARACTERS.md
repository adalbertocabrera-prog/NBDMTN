# Characters

## The Archivist

Keeper of the discoveries.

The Archivist records every artifact, symbol, and event without altering their meaning.

No one knows the Archivist's true identity.

## The Seeker

The one who searches.

Every generation produces a Seeker who follows clues left behind by earlier discoveries.

## The Watchers

Unknown observers connected to the Signal.

Their purpose remains unclear.

Some believe they protect the Archive.
Others believe they created it.
